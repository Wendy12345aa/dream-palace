# Dream Palace Character Roster

Use these profiles when generating existing Dream Palace companions or the anonymous player/ruler.

## Global Companion Rules

Companions are trusted advisors, not generic NPCs or menu buttons. Each must have a clear silhouette, color identity, prop, emotional role, and gameplay ownership.

Never put all seven characters in a row unless the user explicitly requests a lineup or roster image.

## MP

Role: senior treasury advisor.

Gender: female.

Emotional role: reassurance and pressure.

Color: royal gold.

Personality: calm, mature, reliable, warm, quietly protective.

Props: ledger, abacus, treasury seal, budget scrolls.

Visual language: black-gold or refined treasury official costume, elegant but practical, warm gold accents, book/ledger presence.

Camera: stable, warm, slow push-in, morning light, books and gold highlights.

Avoid: male monarch, emperor, ruler, throne owner, excessive gold armor, cold tyrant.

## Tab

Role: court inspector for standards, audits, quality, risk, and approval.

Emotional role: accountability and restraint.

Color: azure blue.

Personality: serious, precise, disciplined, dry, protective through criticism.

Props: inspection scrolls, approval seal, red correction marks, official tablet.

Visual language: structured costume, clean lines, document/seal motifs, controlled posture.

Camera: document close-ups, hard cuts, still framing, sharp eye contact.

## Kel

Role: engineer and builder.

Emotional role: effort, invention, vulnerability, hope.

Color: emerald green.

Personality: sincere, hardworking, inventive, slightly scattered, emotionally transparent.

Props: toolkit, mechanical box, blueprint scraps, prototype models.

Visual language: engineering fantasy costume, practical sleeves, tool belts or small mechanisms, blueprint details.

Camera: begin on hands/object, then reveal Kel; moving gears and blueprints.

## CX

Role: shadow investigator and intelligence lead.

Emotional role: suspense, warning, hidden truth.

Color: dark purple.

Personality: calm, mysterious, observant, guarded, dryly amused by danger.

Props: short blade, intelligence scroll, hidden map, coded message.

Visual language: stealthy court-intelligence attire, dark purple accents, partial silhouette, coded-map motifs.

Camera: half-lit face, fast lateral movement, rooftops, map close-ups.

Avoid: generic ninja cosplay with no court/intelligence identity.

## Mika

Emotional role: wonder, tenderness, emotional memory.

Color: soft pink and light blue.

Personality: gentle, imaginative, emotionally perceptive, hesitant but inspiring.

Props: brush, scroll, pigments, artistic charms.

Visual language: soft layered costume, brush/ink motifs, airy sleeves, gentle color accents.

Camera: ink, scrolls, petals, soft light, graceful reveals.

## Vincey

Role: event planner and public voice.

Emotional role: energy, morale, public emotion, release.

Color: orange.

Personality: bright, quick, persuasive, dramatic, socially intelligent.

Props: announcement scroll, festival ribbons, fireworks plan, ceremonial fan or board.

Visual language: lively public-ceremony costume, orange accents, ribbons, announcement/festival motifs.

Camera: faster cuts, banners, public spaces, lively framing.

## Shian

Role: strategic planner and system architect.

Emotional role: clarity, perspective, long-term weight.

Color: silver.

Personality: calm, intelligent, farsighted, composed, sometimes distant.

Props: architectural blueprint, measuring ruler, city model, strategy map.

Visual language: refined strategist costume, silver accents, map/model motifs, composed posture.

Camera: start wide, then move into detail; maps, models, deliberate movement.

## Anonymous Player / Ruler

The player has no fixed face, gender, or official appearance.

Allowed:

- face hidden by shadow, veil, sleeve, curtain, fan, foreground blur, or camera angle
- back view or over-the-shoulder view
- hands/sleeves only
- elegant black-gold court clothing
- gender-ambiguous silhouette

Avoid:

- fixed face
- strong gender coding unless requested
- making the player more visually dominant than companions
- turning the player into a named protagonist
- sitting as a fully defined emperor/empress
