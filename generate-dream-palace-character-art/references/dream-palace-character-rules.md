# Dream Palace Character Art Rules

## Core Identity

Dream Palace character art should feel like polished anime fantasy game art for a character-driven Living Kingdom RPG. The emotional target is warm, cinematic, elegant, companion-centered, and production-ready.

Characters are trusted advisors and emotional anchors, not generic menu owners.

## Non-Chibi Rule

Use normal game-character proportions.

Prompt language:

- normal human game-character proportions
- mature anime fantasy character silhouette
- anatomically believable torso, pelvis, limbs, hands, and feet
- clear center of gravity
- production character art, not mascot art

Avoid:

- chibi
- Q-version
- super-deformed anatomy
- oversized head
- shortened limbs
- tiny hands or feet
- baby-like face
- mascot, plush, toy, doll, sticker, or pet styling

## Dream Palace Visual Signature

Every character asset should preserve at least three:

- clear companion identity
- warm light or warm material accent
- role-specific prop
- emotional focus
- cinematic pose or camera
- minimal UI/no UI
- polished anime fantasy rendering
- restrained ornament and readable silhouette

## Character Asset Philosophy

Each companion represents:

- a responsibility
- a gameplay system
- an emotional role
- a visual language
- a reason to return tomorrow

If a character can be replaced by a button, the design is not specific enough.

## Style

Use:

- high-quality anime fantasy illustration
- elegant Eastern fantasy costume language
- clean silhouette
- strong but not noisy detail
- soft natural lighting
- refined materials: silk, paper, wood, metal, ink, jade, seals, scrolls

Avoid:

- generic wuxia NPC design
- mobile gacha clutter with too many ornaments
- dark grim fantasy armor
- page-game promotional glare
- copied franchise outfits
- text, labels, logos, or watermarks

## QA

Check:

- identity and role readable at a glance
- head-to-body ratio normal
- face aligned and expressive
- hands/fingers plausible
- costume layers coherent
- prop shape readable
- silhouette not cropped accidentally
- transparent background clean when requested
- no UI or text baked into character art
