## 🌸 About

Dream Palace is a character-driven Living Kingdom RPG where every important decision is made together with seven trusted companions.

Rather than operating menus, players govern a living kingdom through people.

Every advisor has a personality.

Every discussion changes the future.

Every decision leaves a mark on the world.

---

## Why Dream Palace?

Many kingdom games focus on numbers.

Many RPGs focus on combat.

Dream Palace focuses on people.

The kingdom exists because its companions exist.

The player remembers conversations before statistics.

That is the experience we want to create.

---

# Core Vision

A kingdom is not built by one ruler.

It is built by the people who choose to stand beside the throne.

The seven companions are not NPCs.

They are the heart of the game.

The throne belongs to the player.

The kingdom belongs to everyone.

---

# Design Philosophy

## Characters First

Characters are more important than systems.

Every feature should strengthen the player's emotional connection with the companions.

---

## Living Kingdom

The kingdom should continue to change even without the player's direct involvement.

Cities grow.

People react.

Resources move.

Politics evolve.

---

## Decisions Matter

Every important decision should leave visible consequences.

Not only numbers.

The world itself should change.

---
