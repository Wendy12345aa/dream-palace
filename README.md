# 🎮 Dream Palace

> **Build a Living Kingdom.**
>
> A character-driven fantasy kingdom management RPG where seven companions stand beside the throne.

---

## 🌸 About

Dream Palace is an original IP that blends:

- 🏯 Eastern Fantasy Kingdom
- 👥 Character-Driven Gameplay
- 🎭 Cinematic Storytelling
- 🗺️ Kingdom Management
- ⚔️ Adventure & Exploration
- 🤝 Factions & Guilds
- 📈 Living Economy
- 🧠 AI-assisted Development

Unlike traditional management games, players do not manage menus.

Players govern a kingdom through people.

---

# Core Vision

A kingdom is not built by one ruler.

It is built by the people who choose to stand beside the throne.

The seven companions are not NPCs.

They are the heart of the game.

---

# Design Philosophy

## Characters First

Characters are more important than systems.

Every feature should strengthen the player's emotional connection with the companions.

---

## Living Kingdom

The kingdom should continue to change even without the player's direct involvement.

Cities grow.

People react.

Resources move.

Politics evolve.

---

## Decisions Matter

Every important decision should leave visible consequences.

Not only numbers.

The world itself should change.

---
