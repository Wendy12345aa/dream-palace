# Dream Palace Gameplay Bible

## Purpose

Define the playable systems that turn the vision into moment-to-moment decisions.

## Core Loops

1. Listen to companions and kingdom signals.
2. Choose priorities through character-led interfaces.
3. Commit resources, trust, and time.
4. Watch consequences emerge in the kingdom.
5. Respond to new opportunities, crises, and relationships.

## System Areas

- Kingdom management through companions.
- Adventure and exploration.
- Faction relationships.
- Living economy.
- Event chains and visible consequences.
- Trust, loyalty, and companion arcs.
