# Chapter 0 — Project Vision

> *Build a world worth remembering.*

---

# Why Dream Palace Exists

Dream Palace was not created to become another kingdom management game.

It was created to explore a different fantasy.

Many kingdom games ask players to optimize numbers.

Many RPGs ask players to defeat enemies.

Dream Palace asks a different question.

> **What kind of kingdom would people genuinely want to return to?**

We believe a kingdom is not defined by its territory.

It is defined by the people who stand beside the throne.

---

# Vision

Dream Palace is a character-driven Living Kingdom RPG.

The kingdom is not the protagonist.

The player is not the protagonist.

The relationships between the player, the companions, and the kingdom are the true protagonist.

The throne belongs to the player.

The kingdom lives through everyone.

---

# Player Fantasy

Dream Palace is built around a single fantasy.

> **"I want to become the kind of leader people willingly choose to follow."**

Players are not expected to become the strongest warrior.

Players are not expected to conquer the largest empire.

Instead, players build trust.

They make difficult decisions.

They guide companions.

They protect the kingdom.

Leadership is earned through responsibility rather than power.

---

# Emotional Goal

When players finish playing Dream Palace, we do not want them to remember numbers.

We want them to remember moments.

We hope players remember:

- the first morning court
- the first difficult decision
- the first companion who trusted them
- the first city they helped rebuild
- the first time the kingdom truly felt alive

Our goal is simple.

Players should feel that they are returning home, not simply logging into a game.

---

# Core Experience

Every game session should feel like another day governing a living kingdom.

Players should experience a repeating emotional rhythm.

Morning Court

↓

Discussion

↓

Decision

↓

Action

↓

World Changes

↓

Return Tomorrow

The player should always feel that their choices matter.

---

# Design Philosophy

Dream Palace follows several principles that guide every future feature.

## Characters First

Characters are more important than mechanics.

Every new system should strengthen the player's relationship with the companions.

---

## Living Kingdom

The kingdom should continue changing even without direct player input.

Cities grow.

Citizens react.

Politics evolve.

Resources move.

The kingdom should always feel alive.

---

## Decisions Matter

Meaningful decisions should leave visible consequences.

The world should remember the player's actions.

The companions should remember them too.

---

## UI Is Character

Menus should never feel detached from the world.

Every important interface belongs to someone.

The player should feel like they are consulting trusted advisors rather than operating software.

---

## Leadership Before Power

Dream Palace is not a fantasy about absolute power.

It is a fantasy about becoming someone worthy of leading others.

---

# Design Non-Negotiables

The following principles should never be compromised.

1. Characters are more important than mechanics.

2. Every system must strengthen the Living Kingdom.

3. Every major interface should belong to a character whenever possible.

4. The kingdom should continue living while the player is away.

5. Visible consequences are more important than hidden calculations.

6. New features should deepen relationships before expanding content.

If a feature violates these principles, the design should be reconsidered.

---

# Success Definition

Dream Palace succeeds when players remember companions before systems.

Dream Palace succeeds when players miss the morning court more than daily rewards.

Dream Palace succeeds when players feel they are returning home instead of simply opening a game.

Success is measured by emotional memory before feature count.

---

# Project Promise

We are not building menus.

We are building companions.

We are not building only a kingdom.

We are building a world worth remembering.

---

> **Dream Palace is not a game about ruling a kingdom.**

> **It is a game about building a kingdom worth returning to.**
