# Chapter 4 — Character Bible

## Purpose

Collect the companion cast, their responsibilities, emotional arcs, gameplay roles, relationships, and interface ownership.

## Character Design Questions

- What does this companion want from the kingdom?
- What do they fear losing?
- How does their interface express their personality?
- How does the player earn, damage, and repair trust with them?
