# Dream Palace World Bible

## Purpose

Define the kingdom, surrounding regions, cultures, factions, history, economy, magic, conflicts, and living-world simulation rules.

## World Pillars

- Eastern fantasy kingdom foundations.
- Political and social change made visible in the world.
- Factions with motivations, pressure, alliances, and memory.
- A kingdom that grows through people rather than menu optimization.

## Open Questions

- What is the kingdom called?
- What crisis begins the first morning court?
- Which factions support the throne, and which oppose it?
- What makes this world impossible to forget?
