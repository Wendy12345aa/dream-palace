# Chapter 7 — Demo Bible

## Purpose

Scope the first playable web demo: **The First Morning Court**.

## Target Experience

- Length: 15–20 minutes.
- Goal: introduce the throne, the seven companions, the first kingdom decision, and one visible consequence.
- Tone: cinematic, emotional, hopeful, politically charged.

## Demo Beats

1. Opening arrival at the morning court.
2. Companion introductions through their domains.
3. First crisis presented by competing viewpoints.
4. Player decision with tradeoffs.
5. Immediate court reaction.
6. Closing world-state change that invites replay.
