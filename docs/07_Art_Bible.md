# Dream Palace Art Bible

## Purpose

Capture the visual identity of Dream Palace while borrowing philosophy, not visual style, from its inspirations.

## Art Direction Pillars

- Eastern fantasy kingdom atmosphere.
- Character-forward presentation.
- Cinematic court scenes and expressive companion moments.
- A living world that changes visibly after decisions.

## Reference Philosophy

Inspirations may guide ambition, pacing, clarity, emotional presentation, or polish, but Dream Palace must remain an original IP with its own visual identity.
